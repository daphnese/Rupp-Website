    HOME >> Faculty of Social Science and Humanities

      The Faculty of Social Sciences and Humanities (FSSH) at the Royal University of Phnom Penh (RUPP) is one of Cambodia’s leading institutions for education and research in social sciences and humanities. Established to provide comprehensive knowledge and skills, the faculty plays a vital role in addressing Cambodia's social, cultural, and developmental challenges.

      Key Departments and Programs:

    • History
    • Khmer Literature
    • International Business Management
    • Media and Communication
    • Philosophy
    • Psychology
    • Sociology
    • Social Work
    • Tourism
