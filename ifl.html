    Institute of Foreign Languages

    The Institute of Foreign Languages (IFL), is the part of the Royal University of Phnom Penh (RUPP), was established in 1985 to address Cambodia's growing need for foreign language education after the Khmer Rouge era.IFL expanded to include English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and other languages, reflecting Cambodia's global integration.It has become a key center for fostering cultural exchange and training multilingual professionals who contribute to Cambodia’s diplomacy, education, and commerce on the global stage.
